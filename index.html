About Hermes Publisher

Hermes Publisher is a web application that allows creators to upload and manage TikTok videos using the official TikTok API. Users authenticate with TikTok and can securely publish videos to their own accounts.

Privacy Policy

We only access the permissions required to publish videos after the user grants permission through TikTok OAuth. No personal information is sold or shared with third parties.

Terms of Service

Hermes Publisher is intended only for users publishing content to their own TikTok accounts. Users remain responsible for all uploaded content.
